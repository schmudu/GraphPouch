Equations
Equations can be added to graphs on your worksheet using the Properties Panel interface.
Equations
Graph with equation y=x2

  • Add Equation
    To add an equation to a graph follow these steps:
    1. Click with the left mouse button the graph you would like to add an equation to. Optionally you can select multiple graphs by clicking on several graphs while holding the Shift or ⌘ key.

    2. Click the "+" button in the Equations section located in the Properties Panel.
      Equations section of the Properties Panel
    3. In the Equation drop down enter the equation you would like to graph.
      Equations dropdown

      After entering your equation press "Submit".

      See this article to find out more about the types of equations GraphPouch recognizes.
  • Inequalities
    You can also graph inequalities in GraphPouch
    1. Click with the left mouse button the graph you would like to add an equation to. Optionally you can select multiple graphs by clicking on several graphs while holding the Shift or ⌘ key.

    2. Click the "+" button in the Equations section located in the Property Panel.
      Select inequality
    3. Click on the y= button and select the inequality you would like to graph
    4. In the Equation drop down enter the equation you would like to graph.
      Equation inequality options

    5. Change the color and alpha color to the desired settings. An alpha of 1.0 will show a color that is completely opaque and and alpha of 0.0 will be completely transparent. After entering your equation press "Submit".
  • Remove Equation
    To remove a equation follow these steps:
    1. Click with the left mouse button the graph you would like to remove. Optionally you can select multiple equations by clicking on several graphs while holding the Shift or ⌘ key.

    2. Click the "-" button in the Equations section located in the Properties Panel or press the Delete key.
      Equation selected in Properties Panel
