Pages
Pages are the canvas of your worksheet.

You can add as many pages as you like to your worksheet using the instructions below.
  • Add Page
    To add a page to your worksheet use any of the following methods:
    • In GraphPouch, press Control-P (^P).
    • In GraphPouch select Pages > New Page.
    • Press the New Page button
  • Reorder Pages
    At any point in time you can reorder your pages by dragging pages to another position in the Pages Panel.
    1. Select the page you would like to reorder in the Pages Panel by clicking the left mouse button over the page you want to reorder. After selecting the page there should be a grey box surrounding the page.

      Optionally you can select multiple pages by clicking on several pages while holding the Shift or ⌘ key.

      Page selected in Pages Panel
    2. Drag the page(s) to their new position.
  • Remove Page
    To remove a page from your worksheet you must:
    1. Select the page you would like to delete in the Pages Panel by clicking the left mouse button over the page you want to delete. After selecting the page there should be a grey box surrounding the page.

      Optionally you can select multiple pages by clicking on several pages while holding the Shift or ⌘ key.

      Page selected in Pages Panel
    2. Press the Delete key.
